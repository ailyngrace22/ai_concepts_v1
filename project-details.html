Price Inquiry Flowchart

Interface

This is a portion of price inquiry's interface. With the barcode scanner, customer scans the product barcode and the system shall find the barcode on the topspeed file. If the barcode is found under promo period, both the regular and sale price shall be displayed. However, if the barcode scanned was not found, the system displays "Not Found".

One of the interesting logic I find upon creating this project is the conversion of clarion date upon checking whether the barcode is on promo period. The date from the topspeed file is in clarion format, thus, a simple logic to convert the date and compare it with current date was implemented.
